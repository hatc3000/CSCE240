Potential arguments in respect to the order from outputs.png are as follows:

name - returns name of representative
region - returns counties representative is responsible from
home address - returns home mailing address
business address - returns business address of representative
home phone - returns home phone numbers of representative
business phone - returns business phone numbers of representative
incorrect argument handling - Response for arguments not currently handled
personal information - returns personal information
committee assignments - returns representative's committee assignments
service in public office - returns representative's service in public office

Not shown in outputs.png
party - returns political party of representative
bills support - Returns bills supported by representative
Vote History - returns history of votes by representative
